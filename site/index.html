06Questions

Asked before you claim.

Is this a donation?
No. DOV is a for-profit Public Benefit Corporation. A claim is a partnership in a field operation — you fund a base, and you get a named, filmed, permanent presence on the map in return. Sponsorships are not tax-deductible, by design.
What do I actually get for $25,000?
One country — claimable exactly once. When the route arrives, your Forward Operating Base is built there, documented on film, and carries your name on the map permanently. You receive the footage of what you built.
What if my country becomes unsafe?
The operations plan handles this explicitly: any country at State-Department Level 4 defers to the 2051 catch-up window, with substitutes prepared. The route flexes; the commitment doesn’t.
What happens if the founder can’t continue?
The mission is enforced at the corporate level, not the personal one: DOV Global PBC’s charter obligates it to the public benefit, the 25-year operations plan is fully documented, and every base built remains. The structure is designed to outlive any single person.
How is this funded day-to-day?
Revenue. The Agency (the voice-prompt auto-compiler) and DOV’s holdings fund operations — sponsorships accelerate the bases, they don’t keep the lights on. That’s the point of the model.
